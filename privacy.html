Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Git The Look Lab collects, uses, and handles information when interacting with third-party APIs and publicly available trend data sources.

Information We Collect

This project does not collect personal data from users.

The application only accesses aggregated trend data from third-party platform APIs, such as trending search topics or category-level insights. These datasets do not contain personally identifiable information.

How the Information Is Used

The collected trend data is used exclusively for research and analytical purposes, including:

  • Analyzing growth patterns in trending topics
  • Identifying emerging consumer interests
  • Testing data science models for trend detection

The data is used internally for experimentation and research only.

Sharing of Information

Git The Look Lab does not sell, distribute, or share collected data with third parties.

All data processing is limited to internal research purposes.

Data Storage

Aggregated trend data retrieved from APIs may be temporarily stored for analysis and time-series research. No personal user information is stored.

Third-Party Services

This project may interact with APIs provided by external platforms, such as Pinterest, for the purpose of retrieving aggregated trend information.

Use of these services is subject to the respective platform's policies and terms of service.
