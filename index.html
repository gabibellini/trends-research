Git The Look Lab

Git The Look Lab is a small research project focused on identifying emerging fashion, lifestyle, and consumer trends using publicly available data from social platforms.

The project aggregates high-level trend signals (such as trending search topics or aggregated interest indicators) and analyzes their growth over time to identify emerging aesthetics and consumer interests.

Purpose of the Project

The application is used for data science experimentation and research related to trend forecasting and cultural analysis. It does not publish, redistribute, or resell platform data.

Platforms Analyzed

  • Pinterest trend topics
  • Public search trends
  • Publicly available social trend signals
